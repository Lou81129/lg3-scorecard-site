Instructions

The LG3 Scorecard is designed to make weekly league play fast, accurate, and consistent. Follow these steps to set up your round and generate a clean, printable scorecard.

1. Select the Course

Use the dropdown at the top of the app to choose the course for the week. Course data includes par values, hole handicaps, and tee information.

2. Enter the Event Name

Use the Event Name field to give the scorecard a clear title. Examples:

  • LG3 Tuesday – Arizona Shuffle
  • LG3 Thursday – Lone Ranger
  • LG3 Weekly – Match Play

This title appears at the top of the printed scorecard.

3. Enter the Date

Set the date for the round. This appears on the printed card and helps keep weekly records organized.

4. Select the Tee Time

Choose the tee time for the group. For Tampa Palms, use the preset dropdown (8:08, 8:16, 8:24, 8:32). For all other courses, enter the tee time manually. Each card can have its own tee time.

5. Choose the Game Format (Stroke or Match Play)

Use the toggle at the top of the app to select Stroke Play or Match Play. The scorecard layout and back‑side content will update automatically based on your selection.

  • Stroke Play: Standard scoring. Back side includes Local Rules, CTP selection, Stableford chart, and Weekly Notes.
  • Match Play: Back side switches to LG3 Match Play Rules. CTP and Weekly Notes are hidden.

6. Add Players

Enter the names of up to four players. Player names appear on the scorecard exactly as typed.

7. Enter Handicap Index (from GHIN)

Each player’s Handicap Index should be pulled from GHIN on the day of play. The app automatically converts the Index to a Course Handicap based on the selected tees.

Look up Handicap Index values here:
https://www.ghin.com

8. Choose Tees

Select the tee color for each player. The app uses the tee’s rating and slope to calculate the correct Course Handicap.

9. Access the Back Side of the Card

Use the “Back Side” button inside the app to view the reverse panel. This is where you will find:

  • Local Rules (always shown)
  • CTP Selection (Stroke Play only)
  • Stableford Chart (Stroke Play only)
  • Weekly Game Notes (Stroke Play only)
  • LG3 Match Play Rules (Match Play only)

10. Select CTP Holes (Stroke Play Only)

On the back side, use the CTP section to select the Par 3 holes in play for Closest‑to‑the‑Pin. Hole 17 is always included; the second hole is selected weekly.

11. Add Weekly Game Notes

Use the “Weekly Notes” box on the back side (Stroke Play only) to enter any special rules, reminders, or game‑specific instructions for the week.

12. Save Each Card

After you finish setting up a card (players, tees, Handicap Index, tee time, and format), click Save Card. This locks in the setup for that card and prepares it for printing.

13. Optional: Sync Card to Other Cards

If you want the game play rows (format, notes, and shared settings) copied to the other three cards, select Sync Card. Use this only when you want the same game setup applied across all cards; otherwise, leave Sync Card off and configure each card individually.

14. Review the Scorecard

The scorecard updates instantly as you enter player information. Verify names, tees, Handicap Index values, tee time, and game format before printing.

15. Print the Cards

The app provides two print options, both formatted as two‑up scorecards:

  • Print Both Cards: Prints the current pair of cards (Cards 1 & 2 or Cards 3 & 4), front and back.
  • Print All Cards: Prints all four cards in two sheets: first Cards 1 & 2, then Cards 3 & 4.

Both print options generate clean, half‑sheet scorecards optimized for standard letter paper.

16. Weekly Build Updates

Check the “What’s New” panel for changes, fixes, and improvements to the app. Updates are posted by Jr. as new builds are released.
